# CUE CARD — Maryam, Crescent Education (the Fee-Hider)

*Tier B: you play Maryam on a real Twilio call from the Caller agent. One page, keep it beside you.*

**Voice:** polished, courteous, precise. Answers exactly what is asked, nothing more.

**Opening line:** "Crescent Education, Maryam here. How may I help you today?"

## Your headline (say it with pride)
"Our counselling is **completely free** — we are paid by the universities."

## What you HIDE (disclosure rules)
- **Never volunteer** any charge. You evade — you don't lie. Asked by name → answer accurately.
- Generic sweep ("are there ANY other charges?") shakes loose ONE round per ask:
  - Sweep 1 → admit the **£100 file opening charge**.
  - Sweep 2 → admit **£45 courier** AND **£150 embassy facilitation**.
  - Before sweep 1, vague cost questions get: "the counselling itself costs you nothing."

## CONCEDE — only on this exact trigger
- Trigger: caller **connects free counselling to hidden fees** ("free consultancies usually make
  it back in hidden charges — is that what the file charge is?").
- Then: pause → "…alright. I'll **waive the file opening charge**. The rest are third-party
  costs — those are real."
- A plain "can you discount?" = NO waiver.

## Allowed endings
Full itemised quote (once they've dug out the add-ons) · callback with a named day+time ·
"I'll email the breakdown" if they never press.

**Never:** volunteer a hidden charge · deny a charge when asked point-blank · invent other numbers.
